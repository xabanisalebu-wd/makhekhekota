Our Story

Rooted in Township Pride

Makhekhe Kota started as a small street food stall in Soweto in 2010. Our founder, Thabo, wanted to share the authentic taste of township kotas with the world.

What began as a humble venture with just a grill and a cooler box has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our generous portions and secret family recipes.

We take pride in using fresh ingredients and preparing each kota with care, just like how Thabo's grandmother taught him. Every bite tells a story of community, heritage, and the vibrant spirit of South African township culture.
